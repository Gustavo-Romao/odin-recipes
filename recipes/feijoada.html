Feijoada

Description:

A hearty stew featuring pork and black beans. The dish is consumed throughout the country (traditionally on sunday), and every family has their own, special recipe.

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg of black beans
  • 100 g of dried meat
  • 70 g of pig ear
  • 70 g of pork tail
  • 70 g of pig's feet
  • 100 g of pork ribs
  • 50 g pork loin
  • 100 g of paio
  • 150 g Portuguese sausage

Seasoning:

  • 2 large chopped onions
  • 1 bunch of chopped green onions
  • 3 bay leaves
  • 6 cloves of garlic
  • Black pepper to taste
  • 1 or 2 oranges
  • 40 ml of drip
  • Salt if needed
  • Method of preparation

Method of preparation

Soak the meat for 36 hours or more, change the water several times, if it's a hot environment or summer, add ice on top or in cold layers. Cook step by step: the hard meats, then the soft meats . When soft, add the beans and remove the meat. Finally season the beans.
